Concept

For assisted living homes and families of elderly citizens, Vintage Vitality is a health-tracking wearable device that delivers peace of mind, efficiency, and independence. VinVit tracks vital signs including heart rate, blood pressure, and respiration, sending notifications through the companion mobile application and making emergency calls when necessary. The device also vibrates to help senior citizens remember to take medications and tracks their GPS location to prevent wandering off. With its unobtrusive and automated tracking capacity, VinVit helps the elderly stay connected to their caretakers and loved ones.

Health

Using various sensors built into the smartband, such as a pulse oximeter and a mini-sphygmomanometer, the VinVit is able to keep track of a user's primary vital signs: heart rate, blood pressure, and respiration.

Safety

When the VinVit detects unusual spikes, it will refer to the elderly user's contacts. Depending on the severity, the device may contact the user's family, doctor, and/or the local emergency department.

Independence

With the device's built-in GPS and pills reminder system, elderly residents are able to achieve independence. With nothing but a non-intrusive smartband on their wrists, users can enjoy a sense of security and freedom.

Technology

The Vintage Vitality system includes both a hardware and software componenet. The actual device itself is a smartband that goes around the wrist of the person being monitored. Meanwhile, the app allows others to keep track.

Health

The built-in sensors on the smartband allow family members to keep track of their beloved's vital signs from anywhere around the world. The app displays real-time heart rate, blood pressure, and respiration.

Location

In case the elderly user ever gets lost or wanders, the smartband has a built-in GPS tracker that allows family members to track down their beloved if anything goes wrong. They can stay connected 24/7.

Pills

Many elderly people have an array of prescription medications that they are required to take. Our application reminds them at certain times to take medication, glowing different colors for different medicines.
